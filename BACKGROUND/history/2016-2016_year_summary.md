*Source: tcbmag.com (2018 retrospective), security-banks.com*

## Event Details

## What We Know
- Continued growth from 2015; still building toward 200+ events
- Security Bank & Trust began participating (they mention 2017 as their start year for panels, suggesting they were attendees before then)
- By 2018 (year 5), events had roughly doubled year-over-year — implying 2016 was around 100 events

## Context
The 2019 TCB article describes the event as being "in its sixth year" (confirming 2014 founding). By 2016 (year 3), the event had grown beyond pure tech to include:
- Healthcare track (emerging)
- Sales & marketing track (emerging)
- Food and agriculture (emerging)
