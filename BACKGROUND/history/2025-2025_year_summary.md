# TCSW 2025 — Year 12 Summary ("LARGEST STARTUP WEEK IN THE MIDWEST")
*Source: start-midwest.com, twincitiescollective.com, communiful.com, minnestar.org, blog.twincitiesstartupweek.com*

## Event Details
- **Year #:** 12th Annual
- **Dates:** October 6–10, 2025 (Monday–Friday)
- **Format:** 5 days, multi-venue Minneapolis + St. Paul
- **Co-Hosts:** BETA + Communiful
- **Tagline:** "By the community, for the community"
- **Claimed:** "Largest startup week in the Midwest"
- **Attendance:** 1,000+ directly engaged (per Start Midwest recap)

## Day-by-Day Locations
| Day | Location | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Monday, Oct 6 | Distributed / University of MN (MN Cup) | Partner events + MN Cup Grand Finale |
| Tuesday, Oct 7 | Downtown Minneapolis — LaSalle Plaza | Main sessions + Innovation Crawl evening |
| Wednesday, Oct 8 | North Loop | Pitch events + BETA × gener8tor Showcase |
| Thursday, Oct 9 | Downtown St. Paul | Minnedemo + DEED Resource Fair |
| Friday, Oct 10 | Distributed | Closing Party |
