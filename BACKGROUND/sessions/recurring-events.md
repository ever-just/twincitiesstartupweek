# TCSW Recurring Signature Events
*Events that have appeared across multiple TCSW years*

---

## FLAGSHIP / PERENNIAL EVENTS

### MN Cup Grand Finale
- **Type:** Startup pitch competition awards
- **Years:** Annual since ~2014 (co-located with TCSW opening day)
- **Operator:** University of Minnesota (independent from BETA but co-located)
- **2025 Details:** $400K+ total; $100K Grand Prize (AcQumen Medical), $40K Runner-Up (Swinergy)
- **Historical Venue:** McNamara Alumni Center (confirmed 2017, 2018); varies
- **Significance:** Flagship anchor event; drives media coverage for all of TCSW week

### Minnedemo
- **Type:** Rapid-fire product demonstration showcase
- **Years:** Consistent since at least 2017 (11+ years as of 2025)
- **Format:** Startups demo products in rapid succession (TED-style)
- **2025 Location:** Downtown St. Paul (Thursday)
- **Notes:** Fan-favorite; described as "flagship" by multiple sources

### BETA Showcase
- **Type:** Science fair for startups
- **Years:** Annual (also runs in January separate from TCSW)
- **Format:** Startups table-display style; investors + community walk through
- **2023 Notable:** Bus Stop Mamas (Mary Kay) won Golden iPod → seed round
- **2025:** Merged into "BETA × gener8tor Showcase" (200+ attendees)

### Women's Pitch Fest / Women's Competition
- **Type:** Pitch competition for women founders
- **Years:** Confirmed 2017 (McNamara Alumni Center); recurring
- **Notes:** Connected to broader TCSW diversity + inclusion track

### BETA Backers
- **Type:** Investor-founder introduction program
- **Years:** 2019 (confirmed with 3M); recurring BETA program
- **Format:** Early-stage MN startups meet investors including corporate VCs
- **2019 Sponsors:** 3M Ventures

## ANNUAL MILESTONE EVENTS

### Opening Night Party / Kickoff
- **Type:** Community social event
- **Format:** Opening celebration; networking
- **2017:** Able Seedhouse

- **2023:** Twin Ignition Keg House (Twin Ignition sponsor)

### Closing Party
- **Type:** End-of-week celebration
- **Format:** Social; award announcements
- **Notes:** 2021 included MN Startup of the Year award at closing event

### Innovation Crawl
- **Type:** Walking tour of innovative organizations
- **Years:** 2025 (confirmed); likely earlier
- **2025:** Co-hosted with Naturally MN Food & Beverage Showcase
- **Format:** 3 offices visited; local food/bev brands showcased
