# Fortune 500 Title Sponsors — TCSW 2019
*First year Fortune 500 companies served as title sponsors*
*Source: medium.com/twin-cities-startup-week (Laura Beier), tcbmag.com*

---

## TARGET CORPORATION

**Role:** Title Sponsor 2019
**Specific Activation:** Hosted Kickoff Event + Retail Track at Target Plaza Commons

**Key Contact (2019):**
- Gene Han, VP New Ventures & Accelerators

**Quote:**
> *"Startups and corporations can work together in a way that is mutually beneficial. We embrace working with startups through our portfolio of accelerator programs and find that they help inject a more entrepreneurial culture and spirit to our teams."*

**Why Target Sponsors TCSW:**
- Corporate innovation / startup partnership programs
- Talent pipeline for retail innovation
- Community presence in Minneapolis HQ market

**Reengagement Notes for 2026:**
- Target HQ is in Minneapolis — natural community sponsor
- Target's accelerator and innovation programs are well-known (Target Accelerators, Target + Techstars)
- Contact: Target Corporate Social Responsibility or Target Ventures team
- Pitch angle: "Return as founding Fortune 500 partner of TCSW's biggest year yet"

## CARGILL

**Role:** Title Sponsor 2019; co-sponsor 2018
**Specific Activation:**
- 2019: Co-sponsored Techstars Farm to Fork; Demo Day October 15 at Techstars venue
- 2018: MANOVA Summit backing; general sponsorship

**Key Contact (2019):**
- Lawrence Wang, Digitalization & Analytics Strategy Lead

**Quote:**
> *"The pace of innovation is so fast that close-knit relationships with early stage founders is required to remain ahead of the competition."*
> *"Companies like ours became what they are today by being innovative — and combining our 154 years of experience and expertise with the agility and entrepreneurial spirit of our startup partners."*

**Why Cargill Sponsors TCSW:**
- AgTech and Food Technology track
- Startup partnership pipeline
- Wayzata, MN HQ — deeply local

**Reengagement Notes for 2026:**
- Strongest fit for Industry Day (Day 2) with AgTech/Food track
- Techstars Farm to Fork may have new cohort — natural co-sponsor
- Contact: Cargill Ventures or Corporate Innovation team

## 3M

**Role:** Title Sponsor 2019; confirmed 2018
**Specific Activation:**
- 2019: Participated in BETA Backers program (investors meet early-stage MN startups)
- 2018: General title sponsorship

**Key Contact (2019):**
- Ben Wright, Director of 3M Ventures

**Quote:**
> *"Twin Cities Startup Week is a great opportunity to collaborate with the local entrepreneurial community. Innovation is at the heart of 3M's culture, so we're excited to meet Minnesota startups and investors who share that spirit."*

**Why 3M Sponsors TCSW:**
- 3M Ventures actively seeks startup partnerships
- MN Fortune 500 — community investment
- BETA Backers program = direct deal flow access

**Reengagement Notes for 2026:**
- AI Innovation Day (Day 3) is a strong fit for 3M's advanced manufacturing / materials science
- BETA Backers program still operational — natural activation vehicle
- Contact: 3M Ventures team in Maplewood, MN
