## ORGANIZATION BACKGROUND

**Operator:** The Beta Group (BETA) — 501(c)(3) nonprofit
- **Founded:** 2013 by Reed Robinson, Ryan Broshar, and Justin Cox
- **HQ:** Minneapolis, MN
- **Brands managed:** beta.mn, twincitiesstartupweek.com / tcstartupweek.com, tech.mn
- **Mission:** Accelerating the success of Minnesota-based technology founders
- **Current Leadership (as of 2026):**

## YEAR-BY-YEAR HISTORY

### 2014 — INAUGURAL YEAR
- **Year #:** 1st annual
- **Events:** ~15 technology-focused events
- **Attendance:** A few thousand attendees
- **Focus:** Technology startups only
- **Notes:** Founded by Nels Pedersen and Reed Robinson. Intentionally intimate, community-driven format. BETA had its first showcase event in January 2014 before TCSW launched.
- **Sources:** tcbmag.com (2018 article), beta.mn/about

---

### 2015 — Year 2
- **Events:** Growing from 2014 baseline
- **Notes:** Expanding beyond purely tech, beginning to add tracks
- **Sources:** Implied from 2018 TCB article noting evolution

### 2018 — Year 5
- **Dates:** October 8–14, 2018
- **Events:** 200+ events (roughly double from 2017)
- **Attendance:** 12,000+ expected attendees
- **Theme:** Expanding industry tracks
- **New Additions:**
  - Food Ag Ideas Week (new conference co-hosted during TCSW)
  - Blacks in Technology Conference (BITCon) — 3 days
  - MANOVA Summit (health care innovations, Minneapolis Convention Center, backed by Mayo Clinic & Walmart)
  - Mobile app launched for personalized schedules
  - Lyft bus shuttle (Minneapolis ↔ St. Paul evenings)
  - Fly-in program (sponsors: Make It. MSP, Branch Messenger)
- **Key People:** Nels Pedersen (co-founder/director), quoted in TCB
- **Industry Tracks:** Healthcare, Sales & Marketing, Food & Agriculture, Technology
- **Security Bank sessions:** "Raising capital across a variety of industries"
- **Sources:** tcbmag.com ("Bigger Than Ever" — Oct 8, 2018), security-banks.com

### 2020 — Year 7 (COVID VIRTUAL YEAR)
- **Dates:** September 7 (began), spread over 3 weeks; in-person closing ceremony in St. Paul
- **Format:** Entirely virtual (COVID-19 pandemic)
- **Platform:** Hopin (virtual conference platform); option for some smaller in-person sessions
- **Events:** 200+ digital sessions
- **Theme:** Conquering "digital fatigue"
- **Key Adaptations:**
  - Sessions spread over 3 weeks to reduce Zoom fatigue
  - All sessions recorded
  - Professional production standards for virtual speakers (lighting, backdrops)
  - Speaker best practices document distributed
- **Production Lead:** Van Sistine (Alexander Skjong referenced in 2023 team)
- **Medium tagline for 2020:** "19,000+ attendees, 200+ events, hundreds of speakers, pitches, demos + more. Showcasing the best from the Startup Capital of the North September 21–25, 2020"
- **Security Bank sessions:** "Building a dream and surviving a crisis"
- **Sources:** tcbmag.com (Dan Niepow, 2020), medium.com/twin-cities-startup-week, security-banks.com
