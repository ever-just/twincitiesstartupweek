# TCSW 2021 — Year 8 Summary (HYBRID RETURN)
*Source: startribune.com (600098488), info.twincitiesstartupweek.com/sponsor*

## Event Details
- **Year #:** 8th Annual
- **Format:** HYBRID (virtual + in-person)
- **Events:** ~200

  - ~40 hybrid
  - ~70 full in-person
- **Attendance:** Nearly **17,000 attendees** (highest recorded for any hybrid/in-person year)
