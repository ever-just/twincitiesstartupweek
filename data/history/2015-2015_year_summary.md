# TCSW 2015 — Year 2 Summary
*Source: tcbmag.com (2018 retrospective)*

## Event Details
- **Year #:** 2nd Annual
- **Format:** In-person
- **Status:** Limited public data available

## What We Know
- Followed the inaugural 2014 year (15 events, few thousand attendees, tech-only)
- In 2015, TCSW was in an early growth phase expanding beyond pure technology
- Security Bank & Trust began participating in TCSW around this period

## Context
The 2018 TCB article traces TCSW's founding to 2014 with 15 events. By 2018 (year 5) there were 200+ events across multiple industry tracks. The growth from 2015–2017 was gradual expansion of both events and community tracks.
