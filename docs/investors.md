# Capital Ecosystem

TCSW sits inside one of the strongest Midwest capital ecosystems, anchored by MN Cup and a tight set of active local VCs.

## MN Cup economics

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Annual prize pool | $400,000+ |
| Distributed since 2005 | $5.8 million |
| Alumni capital raised | $1.1 billion+ |

MN Cup runs through the University of Minnesota and culminates in the Grand Finale, TCSW's opening-day anchor.
