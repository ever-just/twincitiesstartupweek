## Advertising mediums — confirmed coverage history

These are the outlets that have previously covered TCSW and represent the primary channels for earned media.

### Twin Cities Business Magazine — `tcbmag.com`
**Role:** Anchor trade coverage — most consistent annual preview *and* recap  
**Best pitch timing:** 3–4 weeks before event (preview); 1 week after (recap)  
**Contact:** editors@tcbmag.com

| Year | Title | Author | Angle |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2018 | [Twin Cities Startup Week is Bigger Than Ever](https://tcbmag.com/twin-cities-startup-week-is-bigger-than-ever/) | Kate LeRette | 5th year milestone; 200+ events, 12K attendees; founding story |
| 2019 | [How to Navigate Twin Cities Startup Week](https://tcbmag.com/how-to-navigate-twin-cities-startup-week/) | Amanda Ostuni | Fortune 500 title sponsors (Target, Cargill, 3M); geography/tips |
| 2020 | [Organizers Hope to Conquer 'Digital Fatigue'](https://tcbmag.com/twin-cities-startup-week-organizers-hope-to-conquer-digital-fatigue/) | Dan Niepow | COVID pivot; Hopin virtual platform; resilience story |

**Best angles for TCB:** New title sponsors; attendance/event-count milestones; MN Cup winner profile

## Content cadence (observed patterns)

**LinkedIn (primary channel — 2,709 followers)**
- Pattern: event previews → speaker announcements → sponsor reveals → live coverage → post-event recaps
- Best-performing content: speaker announcements, MN Cup winner reveals, sponsor announcements

**Twitter / X**
- Active during event week with live session coverage
- Attendees use `#TCSW` for real-time tweeting
- 2023: "Zero to Global" theme visible in bio and posts

**Medium (official publication)**
- Used for major milestone announcements only
- Consider reviving for 2026 Fortune 500 sponsor reveal

## PR angles & key quotes

**Evergreen angles**
- Growth story: 15 events (2014) → 200+ events, 17,000+ attendees (2019 peak) → focused 1,000+ (2025 relaunch)
- Fortune 500 engagement: Target, Cargill, 3M, Microsoft, AWS, Walmart, Mayo Clinic
- MN Cup Grand Prize: $400K+ awarded; AcQumen Medical ($100K, 2025), Swinergy ($40K runner-up)
- BIPOC entrepreneurship: MANOVA Summit, BITCon
- Virtual-to-in-person evolution (2020–2025)
- Steve Grove (Star Tribune CEO) as keynote + publisher = cross-promotional opportunity
