# Programming

The recurring flagships anchor the week and drive press regardless of marketing spend.

## Signature events

| Event | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **MN Cup Grand Finale** | Pitch competition | $400K+ prizes; U of MN; opening-day anchor |
| **Minnedemo** | Rapid-fire product demos | Fan-favorite flagship, 11+ years |
| **BETA Showcase** | Startup science fair | 500+ guests; also runs in January |
| **Innovation Crawl** | Walking tour of innovators | 2025 paired with Naturally MN showcase |
| **VC Fireside Chat** | Investor panel | TriNet-hosted 2023; standalone 2025 |
| **Groove Pitch Night** | VC portfolio showcase | Groove Capital, 2025 |
| **Kickoff / Closing Party** | Community social | Bookends the week |

## What works (observed)

- Anchor competition (MN Cup) on opening day sets energy and a press hook
- A single main-stage venue centralizes keynote media
- Free co-working space during the week boosts foot traffic
- Geographic clustering by day reduces travel fatigue
- Structured VC + founder networking scores highest on satisfaction
- A flagship closer (Minnedemo) preserves energy through Friday
