# Organization & Leadership

**The Beta Group (BETA)** is the 501(c)(3) nonprofit that operates TCSW. Founded in 2013 in Minneapolis, it manages beta.mn, tcstartupweek.com, and tech.mn (now defunct). Its mission: accelerate the success of Minnesota technology founders.

!!! warning "Continuity risk"
    Three executive-director transitions in six years (Robinson → Behlivan → Eifert) create institutional-memory risk. Each handoff coincided with format shifts.
