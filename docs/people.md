## Coverage notes

Speaker and session data is complete for 2017, 2018, and 2023 (sched.com archives). Other years' schedule pages returned empty or were hosted on platforms (Hopin, Communiful) without scrapable archives, so per-speaker counts understate the full multi-year total.
