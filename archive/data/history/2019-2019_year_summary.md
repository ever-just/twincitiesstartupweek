# TCSW 2019 — Year 6 Summary
*Source: tcbmag.com (Oct 1 2019), medium.com/twin-cities-startup-week, greatermsp.org, mn.gov/deed, security-banks.com*

## Event Details
- **Year #:** 6th Annual
- **Dates:** October 9–16, 2019
- **Events:** 200+
- **Attendance:** 17,000+ (per multiple sources)
- **Distinction:** First year with Fortune 500 title sponsors; first weekend-spanning edition

## New Features This Year
- Events grouped **geographically by topic** (reduced travel fatigue)
- **Mother's rooms** for nursing women at city hubs
- **First weekend-spanning** edition (Oct 9 = Wednesday through Oct 16)
