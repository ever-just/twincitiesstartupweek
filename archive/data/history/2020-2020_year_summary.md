# TCSW 2020 — Year 7 Summary (COVID VIRTUAL YEAR)
*Source: tcbmag.com (Dan Niepow, 2020), medium.com/twin-cities-startup-week, security-banks.com*

## Event Details
- **Year #:** 7th Annual
- **Format:** Entirely VIRTUAL (COVID-19 pandemic)
- **Start Date:** September (spread over ~3 weeks)
- **Closing:** In-person closing ceremony in St. Paul
- **Events:** 200+ digital sessions
- **Attendance:** ~19,000 (per Medium publication OG description)
- **Platform:** Hopin (virtual conference platform)

## Why Virtual
- COVID-19 pandemic forced all in-person events to cancel
- Team pivoted to fully digital format to keep the week alive
- Challenge: overcoming "digital fatigue" among attendees and speakers

## Key Adaptations Made
| Adaptation | Reasoning |
|---|---|
| Sessions spread over 3 weeks | Reduce Zoom/screen fatigue |
| All sessions recorded | Increase accessibility and replay value |
| Professional production standards for speakers | Distributed document on lighting, backdrops, audio |
| Speaker best practices guide | Ensure quality virtual presentations |
| Closing ceremony in-person in St. Paul | Preserve some community gathering element |

## Platform Details
- **Primary Platform:** Hopin
- **Model:** Mix of live virtual sessions + pre-recorded content
- **Recording:** All sessions archived
