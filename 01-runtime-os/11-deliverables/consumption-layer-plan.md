# Consumption Layer Plan

**Document type:** Compression / audience strategy  
**Status:** Working plan  
**Date:** 2026-06-06

---

## 1. Why this layer exists

The Runtime OS now contains the full reasoning system for SMS x BETA x TCSW 2026.

That is useful for execution, but too much for first consumption.

Angela, Linda, and the SMS team need different levels of compression:

- Angela and Linda need a decision-facing brief.
- SMS needs an action-facing brief.
- The full Runtime OS should remain the source of truth and appendix.

The goal is not to reduce the complexity to something simplistic.

The goal is to create the right entry point for each audience.

---

## 2. Core compression principle

> **Do not make people consume the operating system before they understand the decision.**

The full repo answers: what do we know, what do we believe, what have we decided, what must happen next.

The consumption layer answers:

1. What is the situation?
2. What is the proposal?
3. What decision is needed?
4. What happens next?

---

## 3. Audience map

| Audience | Need | Best artifact | Desired outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| Angela | Confidence, clarity, relief, fundraising story | Angela / Linda decision brief | She feels SMS understands the burden and can help carry it |
| Linda / board chair | Risk, structure, governance, approval path | Angela / Linda decision brief | She sees a board-safe operating model |
| SMS team | Context, role clarity, next actions, guardrails | SMS team operating brief | Team knows what is true, what is pending, and what to prepare |
| Full SMS / BETA working group | Source-of-truth execution system | Runtime OS repo | Workstreams become executable |

---

## 4. What Angela and Linda should receive first

Send only the decision-facing brief first:

```text
01-runtime-os/11-deliverables/angela-linda-decision-brief.md
```

Do not send the full proposal or the full repo as the first artifact unless asked.

The first artifact should be readable in under 7 minutes.

It should answer:

- What did SMS understand from Angela?
- What is SMS proposing?
- Why end-to-end production is necessary?
- How BETA is protected?
- What SMS needs authorized next?
- What happens in the first 30 days?

---

## 5. What SMS should receive first

Send the internal team brief:

```text
01-runtime-os/11-deliverables/sms-team-operating-brief.md
```

The team does not need the full board proposal first.

The team needs:

- where we are;
- what is true;
- what is not yet true;
- why this matters;
- each lead's likely lane;
- what not to do yet;
- what to prepare now;
- what is waiting on Monday alignment.

## 7. Compression rule by audience

### Angela / Linda version

Tone:

- calm;
- respectful;
- board-safe;
- structured;
- risk-aware;
- not hype-heavy.

Main line:

> **We are not proposing more ideas for TCSW. We are proposing the operating structure required to make TCSW believable, fundable, and deliverable.**

### SMS team version

Tone:

- direct;
- energizing;
- clear;
- action-oriented;
- honest about unknowns.

Main line:

> **We are preparing to become the production and community experience engine for TCSW 2026, but we do not yet have final authority, budget, or agreement. Prepare the system without overcommitting the team.**

---

## 8. What not to do

Do not send Angela and Linda:

- the entire Runtime OS at once;
- every open question;
- long sponsor tables;
- venue details before the decision frame;
- app-heavy language;
- internal SMS capacity concerns before the partnership model is understood.

Do not send SMS:

- only the board-safe version;
- vague inspiration without role clarity;
- tasks that imply approval has already happened;
- sponsor or venue assignments before authority is clear.

---

## 9. Recommended sequence

### Before Monday

1. Share or speak from the Angela / Linda decision brief.
2. Keep full proposal ready as backup.
3. Do not overload the meeting with every artifact.

### After Monday if aligned

1. Send full proposal.
2. Send SMS team operating brief.
3. Begin 30-day sprint planning.
4. Open venue, funding, and budget workstreams.

### If Monday needs more review

1. Ask what decision artifact Linda needs.
2. Revise decision brief.
3. Hold detailed appendices until requested.

---

## 10. Final compression thesis

> **The Runtime OS is the source of truth. The consumption layer is the entry point.**

Angela and Linda need to understand the decision.

SMS needs to understand the operating posture.

The repo holds the rest.
