## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: The more specific your prompt, the better results
2. **Provide Context**: Explain why you need the information
3. **Ask for Format**: Specify CSV, JSON, or markdown format
4. **Request Sources**: Always ask for source citations
5. **Follow Up**: Ask clarifying questions
6. **Verify**: Cross-reference findings across sources
7. **Iterate**: Use results to refine next searches
8. **Document**: Keep track of what's been researched
