# MN Cup — Historical Data & TCSW Connection
*Minnesota's premier startup competition; Grand Finale held at TCSW annually*

---

## Program Overview
- **Full Name:** Minnesota Cup (MN Cup)
- **Operator:** University of Minnesota (Carlson School of Management)
- **Type:** Annual startup pitch competition
- **TCSW Relationship:** Grand Finale is a flagship TCSW anchor event on opening day
- **Website:** mncup.org

## Key Statistics
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Founded | ~2005 |
| Total Distributed Since Founding | $5.8 million+ |
| 2025 Total Prize Pool | $400,000+ |
| Alumni Additional Capital Raised | $1.1 billion+ |

## 2025 MN Cup Results
| Place | Winner | Prize | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Grand Prize | AcQumen Medical | $100,000 | Ultrasound monitoring for critically ill infants' heart function |
| Runner-Up | Swinergy | $40,000 | Livestock waste → renewable natural gas + fertilizer |

## Competition Structure
- Open to MN-based startups at any stage
- Tracks typically include: General, Medical, Social Impact, Food/Ag, Student, early-stage
- Semi-finals held in summer; Grand Finale held at TCSW opening day

## Strategic Importance for TCSW
- MN Cup is independently operated but permanently linked to TCSW's opening day
- Press coverage of MN Cup winner drives organic TCSW media coverage
- Winner announcement creates a "hero story" for TCSW's entire week
- AcQumen Medical (2025): Groove Capital portfolio + MN Cup winner = cross-ecosystem credibility
