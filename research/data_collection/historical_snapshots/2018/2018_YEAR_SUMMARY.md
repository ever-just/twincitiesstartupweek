# TCSW 2018 — Year 5 Summary
*Source: tcbmag.com ("Twin Cities Startup Week is Bigger Than Ever", Oct 8 2018), minnestar.org, security-banks.com*

## Event Details
- **Year #:** 5th Annual
- **Dates:** October 8–14, 2018
- **Opening Night:** Sunday evening (Oct 7)
- **Events:** 200+ (roughly double from 2017)
- **Attendance:** 12,000+ expected

## New Additions This Year
1. **Food Ag Ideas Week** — New co-located conference (Mon–Fri)
2. **Blacks in Technology Conference (BITCon)** — 3 days; new; national conference
3. **MANOVA Summit** — Health care innovations at Minneapolis Convention Center (Mon–Wed)
4. **Mobile app** — Launched for personalized event schedules
5. **Lyft bus shuttle** — Minneapolis ↔ St. Paul evenings
6. **Fly-In Program** — Non-MN job seekers flown in (sponsors: Make It. MSP + Branch Messenger)
